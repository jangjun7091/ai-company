# Research

Investigation that informs the company but is not yet founder truth: market and competitive analysis, literature and standards review, domain studies, and raw customer-research findings. This is where the Customer & Market Research agent (`company/agents/customer-research.yaml`) keeps its evidence log.

Conventions:

- Organize by domain or topic. For domain-heavy work, use a subfolder — e.g. `docs/research/<domain>/`.
- Each note uses `templates/research-note.md`.
- Distinguish confirmed findings from hypotheses, and cite sources with their date and reliability.
- Research **informs** founder documents and specs; it is not itself founder truth. When a finding is confirmed and load-bearing, promote its distilled form into `company/founder/` or a spec, and leave the investigation trail here. Open questions that need a decision become entries in `docs/decisions/`.
